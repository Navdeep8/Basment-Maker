Who We are

Your new basement should be everything you dreamed of and more. That’s where we come in. Our team is comprised of esteemed project managers, engineers and designers who work cohesively to create breathtaking basements. We are highly knowledgeable about building code requirements and is known by municipalities in the Calgary as the team that strictly adheres to rules and regulations. As a result, we are able to get building permits approved for our clients much quicker than other contractors can. We handle every aspect of your renovation in-house which means we do all the heavy lifting so the process is as smooth as possible for you

Renovating a basement is more than construction and design. It takes innovation and creativity. The Basement Makers specializes in merging fine detail with exquisite design and structural integrity to achieve stellar results. We use advanced technology to create elaborate virtual tours that allow our clients to see exactly how their new basement will look when completed. Most contractors are focused on getting the job done as quickly and simply as possible. In their attempt to maximize their profit margins, they often cut corners and shortchange homeowners. Not us. The Basement Makers takes a customer-focused approach to renovation projects and offers comprehensive solutions that keep homeowners at the center.
